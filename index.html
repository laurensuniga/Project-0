This Means War!!

  • You are Player 2.
  • To start the game press the "Let's Play" button.
  • Use the flip card button to flip yours and the computer cards simultaneously.
  • The high card wins and collects both played cards.
  • If the two played cards are the same then each player plays two more cards, one face down and one face up.
  • The higher card after the two cards are played then wins all the cards that were just played on the table.
  • The first player to collect all of the cards wins the game.
